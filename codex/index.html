The Codex

A growing collection of Realms and the Enwella within them — local heritage documented and encyclopedic, waiting to be discovered.

Browse the Realms

Each Realm represents a parish or area with its own history, character, and hidden places. Select a Realm to explore its Enwella — the pubs, landmarks, memorials, and forgotten corners that make each place unique.
