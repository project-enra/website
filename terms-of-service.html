2. Description of Service

Veilglass is a location-based mobile exploration game that allows users to:

  • Explore real-world locations and "clear fog" on an interactive map
  • Track their movement and create a visual journal of places visited
  • Discover and collect Points of Interest (POIs) based on their location
  • Access premium features through optional subscriptions

The App requires GPS location access to function and may continue tracking your location even when running in the background or when your device screen is locked.
