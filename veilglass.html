How Veilglass Works

Veilglass transforms the way you experience your surroundings. Through location-based discovery, the app reveals hidden cultural treasures — from forgotten castles and sacred groves to traditional pubs steeped in centuries of stories.

Using authentic geographic data and parish boundaries, Veilglass guides you on a journey through your local heritage, lifting the veil between the everyday and the extraordinary.

Key Features

Fog of War Map

The map starts hidden. As you physically explore, the fog lifts to reveal heritage sites around you — encouraging real-world discovery.

Parish Boundaries

Navigate through authentic administrative boundaries that connect you with centuries of local community heritage and identity.

Heritage Points

Discover castles, churches, historic pubs, monuments, and natural landmarks that tell the story of your local area.
